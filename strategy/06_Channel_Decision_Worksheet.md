## Section E: Quit conditions (write these BEFORE starting, when judgment is clear)

**14. I will pivot the niche if, after 90 days of consistent execution:**

- Median retention is below: ____%
- Email signups per month are below: ____
- And I have published at least: ____ videos

**15. I will SHUT DOWN this channel if, after 6 months:**

- Total followers across platforms are below: ____
- Total revenue is below: $____
- AND I cannot articulate a specific change that would help

(The point of writing these in advance: in month 5, hope and sunk-cost thinking will tell you to keep going. This worksheet is your past self protecting your future self.)
